Here's a roundup of what shipped this month.

## New integrations

We added native integrations for two of our most-requested platforms, making it faster to connect existing help center content without manual re-uploading.

## Improved answer accuracy

Retrieval quality improved across the board this month, particularly for longer documents where the previous chunking approach sometimes missed relevant context.

> [!TIP]
> If you noticed answer quality dip on long-form docs in the past, it's worth re-testing this month — several of the underlying retrieval issues have been resolved.

## Faster response times

Average response latency dropped thanks to backend infrastructure improvements — most conversations now get a first response in under a second.

## Bug fixes

- Fixed an issue where widget theming wouldn't persist after a page refresh in some browsers
- Fixed incorrect timestamp formatting in the analytics dashboard for non-UTC timezones
- Fixed a rare duplicate-message bug in the chat widget under poor network conditions

As always, reach out if you run into anything — feedback from this month's rollout directly shapes what we prioritize next.
