Building an AI chatbot for your website used to require a data science team. Today it takes about five minutes.

## Step 1: Connect your content

Point ContextGPT at your website, docs, or a set of files. It crawls and indexes the content automatically so the chatbot can answer questions grounded in what you actually publish.

## Step 2: Customize the widget

Match the chatbot's colors, avatar, and greeting message to your brand. No code required — everything is configurable from the dashboard.

## Step 3: Embed the script

Copy a single `<script>` tag into your site's `<head>`. That's it — the widget appears on every page.

> [!TIP]
> Start with a narrow set of pages (like your FAQ or help center) before expanding to your full site. It makes it much easier to spot-check answer quality early on.

## Step 4: Test and refine

Ask the chatbot the questions your customers actually ask. Use the analytics dashboard to see which questions it struggled with, then add clarifying content to close the gaps.

```bash
# Example: verify the widget script is live
curl -s https://yourdomain.com | grep contextgpt
```

That's the whole loop — connect, customize, embed, refine. Most teams have a working chatbot answering real questions within the hour.
